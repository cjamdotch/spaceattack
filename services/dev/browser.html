Description

A business directory is a listing of businesses by category that provides information about their products, services, and contact details. Business directories can be found online or in print, and often offer additional features such as user reviews, ratings, and maps. They are useful for consumers looking to find specific businesses or services and for businesses looking to increase their online presence and reach new customers. Some popular examples of business directories include Yelp, Yellow Pages, and Google My Business.
